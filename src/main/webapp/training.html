Innovation Lab Natural Language Processing

The demonstrations and execution of Natural Language Processing on this site is done using the open source Apache framework OpenNLP. Visualizations of the different stages of processing unstructured data help understand what is going on under the hood when text is processed.
Models that are used have been trained by OpenNLP and are freely available on SourceForge.
OpenNLP is an extensible framework, using the base functionality to train your own models can improve results for your specific needs and allow for domain specific terminology to train your own models.
